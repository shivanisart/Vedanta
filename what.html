Shruti - The revealed texts

There are four Vedas... highest knowledge.

Mahavakyas

Mahavakyas are statements... Most Mahavakyas have three components:

  • Tat = "That" = the Infinite = Brahman
  • Enam / Idam / Aham = "This" = the Self = Atman
  • A connector verb (such as "is" or "are")

The realization? The self and the infinite are one and the same.

Example: Tat Tvam Asi
From the Chandogya Upanishad
Chapter 6, Verses 8.8–15

You are that
You are the Infinite
You are Brahman

Because if:
“The essence that gives this Self— you, me, any of us — life is the same infinite power that animates the entire cosmos.”
Then:
The power and potential that created the entire universe lies within us.

That is why there are 108 Upanishads... one central insight:

This is That
The Self is Infinite

Statements that connect the core of our being to the Infinite are called Mahavakyas.
